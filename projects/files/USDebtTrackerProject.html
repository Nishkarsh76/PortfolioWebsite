Forecasting and Trend Analysis for U.S. Public and Private Debt Management

  • Tech Stack: Excel (Advanced Analytics, Forecasting, Visualization)
  • Github URL: Project Link

Problem Statement: The U.S. Government needed a detailed analysis of public debt trends to understand historical growth, identify peak and low debt periods, and forecast future debt for informed policy-making.

Actions Taken:

  • Cleaned and processed historical debt data to ensure accuracy in analysis and forecasting.
  • Analyzed yearly debt percentage increases and identified key fluctuations, including the spike in 2020 due to pandemic-related fiscal policies.
  • Built dynamic Excel dashboards and visualizations to present trends and forecasts to stakeholders.

Results: Identified an average annual debt increase of ~5% (2016-2019), projected public debt to reach $33 trillion by 2027, and provided actionable insights for proactive fiscal planning.
